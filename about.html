About Our Company

We manufacture all type customized eltrical panel board

techno panels is a leading force in the manufacturing industry specializing in customized electrical control panels. Our expertise lies in crafting high-quality, tailored solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients, ranging from Main Switchboards (MSB) and Metering Panels to Control Panels, Automatic Main Failure (AMF) panels, and Automatic Power Factor Correction (APFC) panels.

What sets us apart is our team of highly skilled and dedicated workers who bring a wealth of experience and precision to every project. Our workforce is the backbone of our success, ensuring that each panel is meticulously designed and assembled to meet the highest industry standards. Their commitment to excellence is reflected in the reliability and performance of our products.
